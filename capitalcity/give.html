Strategic Note

Giving Page Structure

Goal: Make it as easy as possible to give, while also educating donors on impact and offering multiple ways to support.

Structure logic:

  • Hero with clear CTA—don't make them scroll to give
  • Impact section shows exactly what gifts accomplish (the $2.40 anchor scales up)
  • Embedded donation form or prominent link
  • Monthly giving pitch (their stated priority for donor retention)
  • Other giving options for planned giving, DAFs, stock, etc.
  • Transparency section reinforces trust (no government funding, accountable)

Recommendation: The donation form should be embedded on-page if possible, not a separate redirect. Every extra click loses donors.
