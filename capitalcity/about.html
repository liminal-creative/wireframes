75 Years of Faithful Service

Since 1949, we've been providing soup, soap, and salvation to those devastated by homelessness in the Capital Region.

From a Storefront to a Beacon of Hope

In 1949, God called a group of Albany businessmen to serve those society had forgotten. They opened a small storefront on Madison Avenue, providing meals and nightly chapel services to men who had lost everything. The annual budget was $3,800.

Today, Capital City Rescue Mission serves as the Capital Region's most comprehensive pathway from homelessness to independence. We provide 900 meals daily, shelter for up to 300 people each night, addiction recovery programs, transitional housing, free medical care, job training, and daily chapel services where over 40,000 people annually hear the hope of Jesus Christ.

What hasn't changed is our commitment to the whole person—body, mind, and spirit.

What We Believe

Mission

We transform lives devastated by homelessness through Christ-centered programs providing food, shelter, and recovery.

Vision

We envision every broken person in our community finding hope, healing, and home.

Our Approach

Christ-Centered Everything

We hold chapel services every single day—one of the only missions in America that still does. Over 40,000 people hear the Word of God at our Mission each year.

Privately Funded Independence

We operate entirely without government funding. This independence allows us to keep Christ at the center of all we do without compromise.

Complete Pathway to Independence

We don't just provide emergency relief. We walk with people through recovery, job training, and transitional housing—their entire journey from brokenness to stability.

Graduates Serving Graduates

Many of our staff completed our programs themselves. They bring lived experience and living proof that transformation is possible.

What Guides Us

Compassion

Every person who walks through our doors experiences the love of Christ through our actions.

Faith

Our Christ-centered approach guides every program, decision, and interaction.

Transformation

Lives change from the inside out when people encounter Jesus and receive comprehensive support.

Relationships

Lasting transformation happens through authentic connections that restore families and create community.

Stewardship

Every donation is handled with integrity to maximize impact for those we serve.
