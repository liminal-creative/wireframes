New Life for Men

A free nine-month residential program for men ready to overcome addiction and rebuild their lives through Christ.

Freedom From Addiction

The New Life Program is a free, nine-month residential recovery program for men struggling with addiction. Through pastoral counseling, Bible study, recovery groups, life skills development, and job placement support, men discover their identity in Christ and build the foundation for lasting sobriety and independence.

Many of our staff are graduates of this program—living proof that transformation is possible.

[Photo of New Life Program]

What to Expect

Duration: Nine months

Cost: Completely free

Housing: Safe, structured residential living on campus

Daily Schedule:

  • Morning devotions and Bible study
  • Recovery groups and counseling
  • Life skills classes
  • Work assignments around campus
  • Evening chapel services

What's Included:

  • Housing and meals
  • Pastoral counseling
  • Addiction recovery support
  • Life skills training
  • GED preparation (if needed)
  • Job readiness and placement assistance
  • Transitional housing opportunity after graduation
[Photo of Transitional Living]

What Comes Next

Graduates of the New Life Program can enter our Transitional Living Program—43 studio apartments where they can live independently while maintaining employment, saving money, and staying connected to the Mission community for ongoing support.
