Free Healthcare for Those in Need

Quality medical care for uninsured individuals—completely free.

The Mission Medical Clinic

Our free medical clinic provides primary care, medications, and specialty appointments for individuals who are uninsured or underinsured. Licensed healthcare professionals and volunteers deliver compassionate, quality care to those who would otherwise go without.

We're one of the only providers of free medical care specifically designed for people experiencing homelessness in the Capital Region.

[Photo of Medical Clinic]

Services Offered

Primary Care Visits

General health assessments and treatment.

Medications

Dispensed on-site at no cost.

Specialty Appointments

Access to specialized care when needed.

Preventive Care

Health screenings and wellness checks.
