The Bridge to Independence

Supportive housing for recovery program graduates building their new lives.

Transitional Living Program

Our Transitional Living Program provides 43 studio apartments for men and women who have completed our recovery programs. Residents live independently while maintaining employment, saving money, and staying connected to the Mission community for ongoing accountability and support.

This is the final step in our continuum of care—the bridge between residential recovery and permanent independence.

[Photo of Transitional Housing]

What to Expect

Housing: Private studio apartment on campus

Requirements:

  • Completion of New Life or New Faith recovery program
  • Maintained sobriety
  • Employment or active job search

Support Provided:

  • Affordable housing while saving for permanent housing
  • Continued connection to Mission community
  • Accountability and encouragement
  • Access to chapel services and fellowship

Support the Full Journey

Your gift funds every step from emergency shelter to independent living.
