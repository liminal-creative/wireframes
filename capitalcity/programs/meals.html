More Than Food

We serve 900 nutritious meals every day to homeless guests and neighbors in need. No ID required. No questions asked. Just a warm meal and a welcoming community.

For many who walk through our doors, a meal is the first point of contact—the beginning of a relationship that can lead to recovery, stability, and a new life.
