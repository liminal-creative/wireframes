Celebrate Recovery

A safe place to find healing through faith and community.

Friday Night Recovery

Celebrate Recovery is a Christ-centered 12-step program for anyone struggling with hurts, habits, or hang-ups. Our Friday night meetings provide a safe, confidential environment where people can explore healing through a growing relationship with God and others who share similar experiences.

You don't have to be a Mission resident to attend. All are welcome.

  • Confidential, judgment-free environment
  • Fellowship and refreshments
