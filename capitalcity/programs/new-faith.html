New Faith for Women

A year-long residential program for women and their children seeking freedom from addiction, trauma, and abuse.

Healing for Women and Families

The New Faith Program provides safe housing and comprehensive support for women recovering from addiction, trauma, and abuse. This year-long program welcomes mothers with their children, offering the stability and care needed to heal as a family.

Through counseling, parenting support, life skills development, and spiritual growth, women discover hope and build the foundation for independent, healthy lives.

[Photo of New Faith Program]

What to Expect

Duration: One year

Cost: Completely free

Housing: Safe apartment-style living for women and their children

What's Included:

  • Private apartment housing
  • Meals and basic necessities
  • Individual and group counseling
  • Trauma recovery support
  • Parenting classes and support
  • Life skills development
  • Education and job readiness
  • Spiritual growth and discipleship
  • Transitional housing opportunity after graduation
[Photo of Mother and Child]

A Safe Place for Children Too

Children are welcome in the New Faith Program. Mothers can focus on their recovery while their children experience safety, stability, and age-appropriate support. Breaking the cycle of addiction and trauma means healing the whole family.

What Comes Next

Graduates can enter our Transitional Living Program, continuing to live in supportive housing while working, saving money, and building toward permanent independence.
