Hope Proclaimed Daily

Over 40,000 people hear the Gospel at the Mission each year.

Daily Chapel Services

We hold chapel services every single day—one of the only rescue missions in America that still does. With speakers from over 200 partner churches, we share the hope of Jesus Christ with everyone who comes through our doors.

For many of our guests, this is the first time they've heard that God loves them and has a plan for their lives. Seeds planted in chapel grow into transformed lives.
