Emergency Shelter

Every night, we provide safe shelter for men and women who have nowhere else to go. Our emergency shelter offers more than a bed—it's a gateway to comprehensive services including meals, clothing, medical care, and recovery programs.

During winter Code Blue nights, we shelter up to 300 people, ensuring no one freezes on the streets of Albany.

Shelter Details

Men's Shelter

60-bed dorm with overflow capacity. Guests can stay up to 40 consecutive nights. Includes showers, meals, and access to all Mission services.

Women's Shelter

12-bed dedicated shelter providing safe overnight accommodations. Includes showers, meals, and a safe daytime environment.

Check-In Time: 5:00 PM daily

Location: 259 South Pearl Street, Albany, NY 12202

No ID required.
