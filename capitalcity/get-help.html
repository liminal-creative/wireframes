Ready for a Fresh Start?

If you're struggling with addiction and ready to change your life, our residential recovery programs can help. We provide safe housing, Christ-centered counseling, life skills training, and a path to independence—all at no cost to you.

New Life Program for Men

A free nine-month residential program for men ready to overcome addiction and rebuild their lives.

New Faith Program for Women

A year-long program for women (and their children) seeking freedom from addiction, trauma, and abuse.

Common Questions

Do I need ID to get help?

No. You don't need identification to receive meals, clothing, or emergency shelter.

Can I bring my children?

Women with children can access our New Faith Program. For emergency family shelter, we can connect you with partner organizations.

Do I have to attend chapel?

Chapel services are offered daily, but attendance is not required to receive meals or emergency shelter.

What if I'm not religious?

Everyone is welcome regardless of faith background. We serve all people with compassion and without conditions.

How do I join a recovery program?

Call us at (518) 462-0459 or visit in person to speak with our intake team.
