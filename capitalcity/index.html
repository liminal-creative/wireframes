Hope Starts Here

For 75 years, we've been transforming lives devastated by homelessness through Christ-centered programs that restore body, mind, and spirit.

More Than a Meal. A New Beginning.

Every night in Albany, hundreds of people sleep outside—trapped in cycles of homelessness, addiction, and poverty. We provide more than emergency relief. Through recovery programs, daily meals, free medical care, and Christ-centered guidance, we walk with people from their darkest moments to lasting independence.

[Photo]

Comprehensive Care for Complete Transformation

From the first meal to independent living, we journey with people through every step of recovery.

Emergency Services

Three daily meals, safe shelter for men and women, free clothing, and medical care for the uninsured—meeting immediate needs with dignity and compassion.

Recovery Programs

Nine-month and year-long residential programs help men and women overcome addiction through pastoral counseling, life skills training, and Christ-centered community.

Transitional Living

Forty-three studio apartments provide stable housing for program graduates as they secure employment and build toward permanent independence.

Support Services

GED preparation, job readiness training, and daily chapel services equip guests with skills and spiritual grounding for lasting success.

From Brokenness to New Life

Ryan arrived at the Mission homeless, addicted, and without hope. Years of trauma and substance abuse had cost him everything—including his relationship with his daughter. Through the New Life Program, he discovered something that changed everything: his identity in Christ.

"The Mission didn't just give me shelter," Ryan says. "They showed me what it means to live as a strong Christian man following Jesus."

Today, Ryan has been sober for over three years. He's working, living responsibly, and getting married. His story is one of thousands made possible by people like you.
