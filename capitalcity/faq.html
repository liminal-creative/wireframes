Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our programs and how to get involved.

For Those Seeking Help

Do I need ID to receive services?

No. You don't need identification to receive meals, clothing, or emergency shelter.

What are your meal times?

Breakfast is served at 7:00 AM, lunch at 12:00 PM, and dinner at 5:00 PM daily.

Can I bring my children?

Women with children can access our New Faith Program. For emergency family shelter, we can connect you with partner organizations that serve families.

Do I have to attend chapel to receive help?

Chapel services are offered daily and are an important part of what we do, but attendance is not required to receive meals or emergency shelter.

How do I join a recovery program?

Call us at (518) 462-0459 or visit in person to speak with our intake team about the New Life Program for men or New Faith Program for women.

How long can I stay in emergency shelter?

Men can stay up to 40 consecutive nights in our emergency shelter. Extended stays may be available through our Work Readiness Program.

For Donors & Supporters

How is my donation used?

Your donation directly funds meals, shelter, recovery programs, medical care, and all the services that help people rebuild their lives. We are privately funded and do not accept government money.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. Capital City Rescue Mission is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. You will receive a receipt for your tax records.

What is the cost per meal?

$2.40 provides one hot, nutritious meal to someone in need.

Can I designate my gift to a specific program?

Yes. Contact us if you'd like to direct your gift to a specific area of ministry.

How can I give monthly?

Visit our Give page and select "Monthly" to become a Hope Partner with a recurring gift.

For Volunteers

How do I sign up to volunteer?

Visit our Volunteer page or call (518) 462-0459 to learn about current opportunities and sign up.

Can my church lead a chapel service?

Yes! We welcome churches to sign up for evening chapel services. Contact us to schedule.

What volunteer opportunities are available?

We need help serving meals, sorting donations, administrative support, and professional skills like medical care and career coaching.

Do I need a background check to volunteer?

Requirements vary by role. Our volunteer coordinator will guide you through the process.

About the Mission

When was Capital City Rescue Mission founded?

We were founded in 1949 by a group of Albany businessmen who felt called to serve those experiencing homelessness.

Do you accept government funding?

No. We are entirely privately funded, which allows us to maintain our Christ-centered mission without compromise.

Are you affiliated with a church or denomination?

We are an independent, interdenominational Christian ministry supported by over 200 churches throughout the Capital Region.

How can I see your financial information?

Visit our Financials page to view our annual reports and financial statements.
