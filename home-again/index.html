01 — Introduction

Dear Nate and the Home Again Team,

We're glad to keep this conversation going and to help you bring clarity to Home Again as you step into this next chapter and head toward your biggest fundraising season of the year.

You're building something rare in Pine Bluff. Homes that sat empty are restored and sold to families at half their market value, turning renters into owners and opening a path to generational wealth in a city where most people have never owned a home. That is a different story than the one Pine Bluff is usually told about itself, and it deserves to be told clearly.

This is the moment to sharpen that story and give supporters simple ways to invest before Giving Tuesday and year-end. What follows is a focused plan to get there by October 1.

Warmly,
The Liminal Team

03 — What We'll Do Together

What We'll Do Together

Listening and Mission Model Canvas

  • We begin by listening to your staff and board, along with the partners who know your work.
  • Then we map Home Again on a single page that captures who you serve and how the work gets funded. This becomes the foundation for everything that follows.

Brand Messaging

  • We define your core message set, from why you exist to the words that carry it.
  • We'll build it around our Four Spaces of Engagement, a framework that fits Home Again's story well.

Visual Identity Refresh

  • We refresh your look and deliver a short brand guide so everything stays consistent.
  • We'll also shape how Home Again, your warm and human parent brand, connects to the Freedom Center as a movement and hub sub-brand, giving both room to grow.

Website Refresh

  • We rebuild your site as a focused 6 to 8 page experience that reflects the new brand and makes it easy to give.
  • This includes an updated Ways to Give page and a fresh Donorbox account set up under Home Again, with one-time, recurring, and donor advised fund options.

Year-End Communications Plan

  • We hand you a plan and starter content for the season across social, direct mail, and your newsletter, timed for Giving Tuesday and December 31.
